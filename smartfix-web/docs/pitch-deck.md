**The Problem:**
- Citizens have no easy way to report civic issues (potholes, water leaks, etc.)
- Government agencies receive scattered reports via multiple channels
- Issues go unresolved for months due to poor prioritization
- No feedback mechanism for citizens about resolution status

**Impact:**
- Safety hazards on streets
- Public frustration with government responsiveness
- Billions in infrastructure costs from preventable damage
